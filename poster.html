Oral Presentation

Research posters summarize information or research concisely and attractively to help publicize it and generate discussion. The poster is usually a mixture of brief text and tables, graphs, pictures, and other presentation formats. Poster presentations are for Ph.D. scholars in their 3rd year who have passed their comprehensive exam. MTech(R) students in their 2nd year must choose one out of either oral or poster presentations. â€‹

Presentation Requirements

The presentation must include:

  • Motivation for the work.
  • Brief literature review.
  • Objective and methodology followed.
  • Results and discussion.
  • Future work.
Submission Requirements

Poster size should be A1 (59.4 cm x 84.1 cm)
