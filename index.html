About AERES 2024

Aerospace Research Symposium (AERES) provides a platform, for the students of the Aerospace Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, to present their research work for future Aeronautics and Space technologies. After a brief hiatus, we are pleased to announce Aerospace Research Students' Symposium 2024 (AERES 2024).

Once in Lifetime Experience

At AERES 2024, we encourage the students to discuss their research work through oral, poster, and video presentations. AERES also provide a platform for various industries to showcase their portfolios and engage students through workshops and keynote speeches.

Enhanced Learning

We cordially invite you all to partake in this 4-days extravaganza. Thank you !
